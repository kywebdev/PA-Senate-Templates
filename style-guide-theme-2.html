Typography

The font families used in this design are Bitter, a serif font used for headings, and Source Sans Pro, a sans-serif font used for body copy. Both are Google Fonts.

Header

Information Bar

The header area is divided into three components. The first one is the information bar, located at the very top of the page. It includes the search bar, e-newsletter sign up button, and social icons.

The information bar does not display on mobile devices. Links from this area can be found in the mobile navigation menu.

Hero Area

The hero area consists of a background image, a headshot of the senator on the left, and a translucent box of essential senator information on the right.
