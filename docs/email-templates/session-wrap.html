Dear Friend,

It was truly an honor and a pleasure to serve as the Chairman of the Senate Veterans Affairs & Emergency Preparedness Committee for the 2015-16 Legislative Session. The Committee was extremely active this past session as we considered a lengthy list of bills that addressed a variety of issues of importance to our military personnel, veterans, first responders and their family members.

We visited military facilities and veterans homes around the Commonwealth and held public hearings around the state to hear from first responders as a way to gather important background information needed to make informed decisions on matters that impact the lives of so many of our fellow Pennsylvanians.

While it would be impossible to discuss every issue that came before the Committee over the past two years, I would like to provide a brief overview and summary of the legislation that was enacted during the 2015-16 Legislative Session.

If you find this e-newsletter useful, I invite you to visit my website http://www.senatorvulakovich.com for more information about your state government. If you do not wish to receive these e-newsletters, please click the “unsubscribe” button at the bottom of the page.

Sincerely,

Randy Vulakovich
